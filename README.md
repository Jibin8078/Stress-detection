Stress is a prevalent issue in the IT industry, impacting the mental and physical health of
professionals. By developing a system for stress detection, organizations can proactively identify
and address stressors, promoting employee well-being and satisfaction High levels of stress can
negatively affect productivity, creativity, and job satisfaction. By detecting stress early, organizations can implement timely interventions, such as workload adjustments or stress
management programs, to maintain high levels of productivity and performance.
